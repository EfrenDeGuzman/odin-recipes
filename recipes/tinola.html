Chicken Tinola

Description

Tinola is a Filipino soup dish that typically consists of chicken, ginger, and vegetables such as green papaya or chayote, and sometimes served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 medium green papaya or chayote, peeled and sliced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until brown on both sides. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, saut√© the ginger, garlic, and onion until fragrant.
  4. Add the green papaya or chayote and chicken broth.
  5. Return the chicken to the pot, and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and let the soup simmer for about 20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
