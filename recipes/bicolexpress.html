Bicol Express

Description

Bicol Express is a spicy Filipino dish that originated from the Bicol region of the Philippines. It is a stew made with tender pork, coconut milk, chili pepper, and other spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork, sliced into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 2-3 bird's eye chili pepper, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong alamang)
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the pork and cook until brown on both sides. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, saut√© the onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced chili pepper and stir for a few seconds.
  5. Stir in the shrimp paste and cook for about a minute.
  6. Stir in the shrimp paste and cook for about a minute.
  7. Bring the stew to a boil, reduce heat, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the pork is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
