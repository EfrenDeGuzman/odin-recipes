Kare-kare

Description

Kare-kare is a Filipino stew made with oxtail, vegetables, and peanuts, and usually served with a savory sauce made from ground peanuts and annatto oil.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs oxtail, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons annatto seeds (optional)
  • 1 cup ground peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 2 bunches of bok choy, chopped
  • 2 medium-sized green beans
  • 2 cups water

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the oxtail pieces and cook until brown on both sides. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the annatto seeds (if using) and let it cook for a minute.
  5. Stir in the ground peanuts and cook for another minute.
  6. Add the beef broth, oxtail, fish sauce, and 2 cups of water to the pot.
  7. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and let it simmer for about 1 1/2 hours or until the oxtail is tender.
  8. Add the eggplants, bok choy, and green beans and continue to simmer for another 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
